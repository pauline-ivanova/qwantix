## Shopping Advertising FAQ {#SearchOptimizationFAQ}

### What's the difference between Shopping ads and text-based search ads?
Shopping ads display product images, prices, and details directly in search results, while text ads only show text. Shopping ads are ideal for e-commerce businesses showcasing specific products.

### How do I optimize my product feed for better performance?
Focus on accurate and detailed product titles, descriptions, and high-quality images. Use relevant keywords, keep pricing up-to-date, and ensure your feed complies with platform guidelines.

### Can I use Shopping ads on platforms other than Google?
Yes, Shopping ads are available on various platforms including Google, Bing, Facebook, and Instagram. Each platform has its own specifications and best practices.

### How do Shopping ads impact my organic search rankings?
While Shopping ads don't directly affect organic rankings, they can complement your SEO efforts by increasing brand visibility and driving traffic to your product pages.

### What metrics should I track for Shopping ad campaigns?
Key metrics include click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, return on ad spend (ROAS), impression share, and average cost-per-click (CPC). Also monitor product-specific performance.

### How often should I update my Shopping ad campaigns?
Regularly update your product feed (at least weekly), adjust bids based on performance, and refresh your campaign structure seasonally or as your product lineup changes.

### Can I use remarketing with Shopping ads?
Yes, you can create remarketing campaigns using Shopping ads to re-engage users who have previously interacted with your products or website.

### How do Shopping ads differ across various e-commerce platforms like Google, Amazon, and Bing?
While all platforms show product images and prices, each has unique features. Google Shopping offers the widest reach, Amazon ads appear directly on the marketplace, and Bing Shopping provides access to the Microsoft network. We tailor strategies to each platform's strengths.
