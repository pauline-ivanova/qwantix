## Display and Social Media Advertising FAQ {#SearchOptimizationFAQ}

### What's the difference between display ads and social media ads?
Display ads appear on websites within ad networks, while social media ads appear on social platforms. Display ads are great for brand awareness and reaching a broad audience, while social media ads offer more precise targeting based on user interests and behaviors.

### How do you measure the success of display and social media campaigns?
Key metrics include impressions, reach, engagement rate, click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, and return on ad spend (ROAS). We also track brand lift and audience growth for social media campaigns.

### What types of visuals work best for display and social media ads?
Eye-catching images, short videos, and interactive formats tend to perform well. The specific format depends on the platform and campaign goals. We recommend A/B testing different visuals to find what resonates best with your audience.

### How do you ensure brand safety in display advertising?
We use advanced targeting options, exclusion lists, and reputable ad networks to ensure your ads appear in brand-safe environments. We also continuously monitor placements and adjust as needed.

### What's the ideal frequency for social media ads?
The ideal frequency varies based on campaign goals and audience behavior. Generally, we aim for a frequency of 3-8 exposures per user over a campaign period to balance reach and avoid ad fatigue.

### How do you leverage user-generated content in social media advertising?
We identify and curate high-quality user-generated content, obtain necessary permissions, and incorporate it into ad campaigns. This approach enhances authenticity and engagement rates.

### Can display and social media ads work together with other marketing efforts?
Absolutely. We integrate display and social media ads with your overall digital strategy, including search ads, email marketing, and content marketing for a cohesive multi-channel approach.
