### SEO Migrations & Recovery

**German Pet Insurance Platform**
*Industry:* Finance & Insurance
*Market:* Germany (EU)

**Challenge:** A large-scale structural update caused an overnight loss of ~80% of organic visibility across commercial pages.
**Action:** Conducted a full technical audit, rebuilt URL architecture, resolved internal linking and indexation issues, and reinforced E-E-A-T signals across key templates.
**Outcome:** Core keywords recovered to Top 3–5 positions, with non-brand organic traffic growing +230% month over month.

---

**International E-commerce Brand**

### E-commerce SEO

**Niche E-commerce Store — SEO for Complex Product Catalogue**
*Industry:* E-commerce
*Market:* EU

**Challenge:** The store operated in a highly specialised niche with a complex product catalogue, including multiple categories, collections, and filter-driven pages. This created risks around diluted SEO signals, overlapping URLs, and unclear prioritisation of commercial pages.

**Action:** Reviewed catalogue structure, category logic, and internal navigation. Defined priority categories, clarified the role of filter-generated pages, and improved internal linking between collections and product pages to better reflect search intent and commercial relevance.

**Outcome:** Clearer catalogue hierarchy, improved SEO clarity for priority categories, and stronger alignment between product structure and transactional search intent in a competitive niche market.
